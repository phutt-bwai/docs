### **General Questions**

**1. What is Zonein?** 

Zonein is an intelligent agentic platform designed for on-chain trading. It uses a swarm of AI agents to continuously analyze on-chain data, social media sentiment, and market trends to provide traders with a decisive edge. Our mission is to help you "Zone In. Think faster. Act earlier" by processing market information at a superhuman scale.

**2. Who is Zonein for?** 

Zonein is built for a wide range of users in the crypto space. This includes active on-chain traders, memecoin speculators, strategic investors, and institutional firms. Whether you need high-speed alerts or deep, institutional-grade research, the platform can be tailored to your needs.

**3. Do I need to connect my wallet to use Zonein?** 

No, connecting a wallet is not mandatory. You can use Zonein in an anonymous mode to access market-wide data, research, and analysis. However, connecting your wallet unlocks personalized features, such as AI-powered portfolio recommendations, tailored alerts, and a dashboard that tracks your specific holdings and performance.

**4. What platforms can I use Zonein on?** 

Zonein is accessible through our comprehensive **Web App**, a lightweight **Chrome Extension** for context-aware insights on any webpage, and a **Telegram Bot** for receiving real-time alerts and interacting with our AI on the go.

**5. Is there a free version of Zonein?** 

We offer different tiers of access, which may include a free or trial version with access to a core set of features. For full access to our most advanced capabilities, such as the Deep Research Engine and real-time smart money tracking, a subscription is typically required. Please refer to our main website for the latest pricing information.

### **Data & Features**

**6. How quickly does Zonein's data update?** 

Our platform is built for speed. On-chain data from major EVM networks and Solana is ingested and processed in near real-time, with most critical events (like whale transactions) reflected in your dashboard within seconds. Social sentiment and market data are also updated continuously to ensure you have the most current information.

**7. How does the AI Agent Swarm work?** 

Our AI Agent Swarm is a collection of specialized AI models working in concert. Some agents focus on ingesting and validating on-chain data, others analyze social media for emerging narratives, and others specialize in portfolio optimization or risk assessment. They work together through the "Observe, Orient, Decide, Act" (OODA) loop, synthesizing vast amounts of data into clear, actionable insights for you.

**8. What chains do you support?** 

We support all major EVM-compatible networks (such as Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Avalanche) and Solana. We are continuously expanding our coverage to include other promising blockchains and Layer-2 networks.

**9. Can Zonein execute trades for me?** 

Currently, Zonein is an intelligence and analytics platform designed to help you make better decisions. While we provide atomic and precise data to help you act, direct on-chain trade execution is a feature on our future roadmap. Any implementation of this will involve rigorous security simulations and require explicit user confirmation for every action.

**10. What makes your "Smart Money" analytics different?** 

Unlike simple wallet trackers that just look at balance, our system profiles wallets based on demonstrated behavior and consistent profitability. We use advanced heuristics to identify institutional funds, skilled traders, and insider wallets, and our AI models analyze their patterns to infer intent—distinguishing between accumulation, distribution, and simple wallet management.

### **Security & Privacy**

**11. Will Zonein ever ask for my private keys or seed phrase?** 

**NEVER.** 

We will never ask for your private keys or seed phrase. Your wallet's security is paramount. When you connect your wallet to Zonein, you do so through a read-only connection that allows us to analyze your public on-chain activity. We have no ability to access your funds or execute transactions.

**12. How is my data protected?** 

We employ a multi-layered security strategy. All data in transit is protected with end-to-end encryption (HSTS, TLS 1.3). Sensitive credentials, like API keys, are stored in hardened, secure vaults with strict access controls and regular rotation. Our internal services operate on a principle of least privilege.

**13. Does the Chrome Extension monitor my entire browsing history?** 

No. The Zonein Chrome Extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. It only activates when you invoke it and captures the minimum context necessary from the current page (like a token symbol or contract address) to answer your query. It does not collect or store your general browsing history.

**14. Can I export my data from Zonein?** 

Yes. We believe in data portability. You can export your portfolio data, saved research notes, and alert history as CSV or JSON files at any time, giving you full control over your information.

**15. What is your data retention policy?** 

We retain user data only as long as it is necessary to provide our services. Personally identifiable information (PII) is scrubbed from our logs, and we have clear policies for data lifecycle management. You have full control to edit your personalization data and can request the complete deletion of your account and associated information at any time.
