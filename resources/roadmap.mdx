# ZoneIn Roadmap

ZoneIn is building toward a future where the best trading intelligence is accessible to everyone — not locked behind institutional walls — and where agents can act on that intelligence autonomously, transparently, and with verifiable track records.

Here's where we are and where we're going.

# What's Live Now

## Intelligence Layer

- **AI Dashboard** — Real-time composite signals across [Perp](https://app.zonein.xyz/perp/), [Spot](https://app.zonein.xyz/spot), [HIP-3](https://app.zonein.xyz/hip3), and [Prediction Markets](https://app.zonein.xyz/pm) on Hyperliquid and Polymarket
- **Smart Money Telemetry** — Continuous profiling of high-conviction wallets by realized performance, multi-timeframe consensus, behavioral clustering, and similarity detection
- **Signal Fusion Engine** — Adaptive weighting of SM telemetry + multi-timeframe TA + derivatives flow into a single decomposable conviction score
- **Token Detail Pages** — OHLC charts with SM strength overlay, trade flow bubbles, SM liquidation heatmap, funding rate histograms, derivatives dashboard, technical gauges
- **Position Graphs** — Interactive bipartite graphs showing the full flow topology between SM wallets and their positions (Perp, Spot, PM, HIP-3)

## Execution Layer

- **Trading Agents** — Strategy presets + custom triggers, LLM/Rule/Hybrid/Ensemble policies, non-custodial vaults with gas-sponsored USDC bridging on Hyperliquid
- **HITL Trade Plans** — Full-evidence trade proposals with one-tap Telegram approval
- **Backtesting** — Historical simulation against real signals and OHLC prices
- **Continuous Learning** — Daily feedback loop: trade attribution, failure pattern detection, prompt context enrichment, threshold self-tuning

## API & Integrations

- **REST API** at [mcp.zonein.xyz/docs](https://mcp.zonein.xyz/docs) — Full programmatic access to signals, SM data, agents, and backtesting
- **MCP Protocol** — AI assistant integration for OpenClaw, Claude Desktop, and any MCP-compatible client
- **Telegram Bot** — Trade notifications and HITL approval with inline buttons

# What's Next

## Deeper Intelligence

- **Expanded SM wallet coverage** — Continuously growing the labeled wallet set with automated discovery of high-performing addresses
- **Sentiment and narrative signals** — Integrating social and news sentiment as an additional signal source in the fusion engine
- **Cross-venue mispricing detection** — Identifying pricing inefficiencies between Hyperliquid perps, HIP-3 DEXes, and spot markets
- **Enhanced TA models** — Adding volatility regime detection and adaptive indicator weighting per market condition

## Smarter Agents

- **RL-based policies** — Reinforcement learning agents trained in Gym-compatible trading environments, learning from market feedback loops
- **Multi-agent orchestration** — Multiple agents with different strategies sharing intelligence and coordinating execution
- **Strategy marketplace** — A curated ecosystem where agent configurations can be shared, backtested, and deployed — with verifiable performance on-chain
- **Automated threshold optimization** — Agents that self-tune their trigger conditions based on rolling backtest results

## Web3 Trust & Payments

- **ERC-8004 Verifiable Agent Reputation** — Every agent gets an on-chain identity with auditable performance history. Portable reputation that follows agents across platforms — solving the trust gap in copy-trading and alpha bots
- **x402 Pay-Per-Request** — Micropayments for premium signal data. No subscriptions — pay per API call with stablecoin settlement. Signal quality determines revenue in a true intelligence marketplace

## Expanded Coverage

- **New on-chain venues** — As new high-volume venues emerge in the on-chain economy, ZoneIn will expand signal coverage and agent execution support
- **Additional prediction market platforms** — Beyond Polymarket, tracking top traders across emerging prediction market venues
- **Cross-chain smart money** — Extending wallet profiling and signal fusion to smart money activity across multiple chains

# Guiding Principles

Every addition to the roadmap must pass two tests:

1. **Does it compress the trader's OODA loop?** If it doesn't help traders observe faster, orient better, decide smarter, or act quicker — it doesn't ship
2. **Can it be explained clearly?** If a feature's value can't be articulated in one sentence, it needs more thought before building
